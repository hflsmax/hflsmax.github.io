2016-12-07

Recently I’m forced to be certain about things, especially about myself. This is tired. I wish she can take me as is.

When I look at her I’m dazzled. And for this I can’t say more, because I can’t translate this into language. And I think, there’re so many ways to live a life, so many possibilities and impossibilities, and yet I just want her. More specifically, I just want her doing nothing, raise her head and look at me, so I can see her. I want to see her. I want to see her, without us being any existences of this world. We are rip of name. We become the only two in this world.

I don’t want to say I love you, for that love is such an ambiguous word. I will coin a word only for me and you, and because we don’t change, the word don’t change. It hangs somewhere in this universe, until we die; until the world die.

Yet I love you.

2016-11-15

Staying in Pittsburgh

Life is really a drama, but yeah, it always is. It’s fun, it’s entertaining, but always believing in the volatility and transience of live, I’m really not surprise by its unpredictability. You know, I belong to everywhere, and I’m about to go anywhere.

Uber ATC called me yesterday, confirming to raise my base salary to 105k. The number meets my expectation. It’s similar to what Google Pittsburgh office offers to a new grad. A friend in Uber San Fransisco office reminds me that Pennsylvania’s income tax rate is only 3.1%, compared to 9.3% in California. The difference in the tax is enough to remove the surplus that I’ll earn in Bay Area, and considering the much cheaper cost of living in Pittsburgh, ATC actually gives me a better package. The over all compensation is not high though, but enough to live a decent life in Pittsburgh. In addition, the most compelling aspect of ATC is its cool projects. It’s like a startup inside a startup. A lot of things are happening there. A whole new picture of automatous vehicle is being drafted.

I’m still wary about making the decision. I told my friends about it, and cautiously posted it on social media. After I explained my reasoning, they all agreed with my thoughts. I also explained my concerns, but while saying it, I gradually resolved them. Everything seems to lead to the conclusion that Uber ATC is a better choice.

Sure, Pittsburgh is a cold, lonely place, but is it necessarily a bad thing? It’s not that cold that I will feel physically uncomfortable, and also other seasons in Pittsburgh is actually pretty nice. Also, there’re many people complain about Pittsburgh being a boring city, but in my experience, most of them aren’t even part of the city. They don’t know much history of Pittsburgh, they haven’t visited all the major places in Pittsburgh, and they don’t participate in the civil activities. It might be true that other cities offer more “fun”, and people who find Pittsburgh boring may find “fun” in other cities. But are they really going to be interested in other cities’ history, places and activities? I don’t know. For me, I’m deeply yet sadly aware that I’m not going to know every thing and every person in this world, so what should I do? “Dig it!” No matter where I am, what I’m looking at, whom I’m talking to. “Dig it!”

2016-11-06

Nov 6

Every day I go to sleep, I think about my position in the world. I think of the Earth; I think of how small it is; I think of how exact my location on the world is. I think of how many people in this world that I won’t be able to know in my short life, and how highly likely it is that I will insanely fall in love with many of them. I think of many stories that I’ve heard, and think of how few of them I can relate myself to. I think of the vastness of this planet, and I think of how trivially easy it is to get to anywhere. I think of many impossibilities, and I think of the fact that they’re actually possible given the fact that I’m immortal and omnipotent. Where should I go and what should I do? I feel so confined when thinking how free I can be. I’m acutely aware that right now I’m pretending being something, because it’s hard not to be something. So what about tomorrow? Should I do the same tomorrow? What is tomorrow? What’s the difference between tomorrow and today? Future and past?

Everyday I think about possibilities outside what I already know. I imagine musics and literatures that don’t make sense at all but are so beautiful that make me cry. I’m not afraid of losing them. They are there; they are safe; they won’t die. Trainings in statistics make me think in high dimensions and make me aware of existence of everything, literary everything. I like to think of songs as lives living in a high dimension, and musicians don’t create them, they find them, the same as explorers discovering a new species on the earth. Sure some songs carry distinct characteristic of the musician, but that doesn’t mean they own the songs; the songs belong to everyone in this world. It’s for the same reason that no one can claim the ownership of a species; it’s just ridiculous to do so.

2016-10-30

Morning

I rarely possess freedom, except when I write. It still needs tons of practice to really write freely. When words are on the paper they solidify, and start their journey to make sense to different people. This is daunting. Because it starts to make sense to me, which it didn’t. As I write more I feel more confined by the stuff that I wrote, and I need to constantly read back to ensure the congruency. It’s not my own thoughts anymore; I feel like another person. I wanna be absolutely sincere about myself and everything in the world. I want people understand me as the true me, my love as the true love of mine.
